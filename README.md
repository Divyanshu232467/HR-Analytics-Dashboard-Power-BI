# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Project

### **Project Overview**
[cite_start]This project analyzes HR data for **1,470 employees** [cite: 8] to identify workforce trends and key factors behind employee attrition. The dashboard provides a visual breakdown of why employees leave the company, focusing on demographics, job roles, and salary distributions.

---

### ** Key Metrics (KPIs)**
* [cite_start]**Total Employees:** 1,470 [cite: 8]
* [cite_start]**Attrition Count:** 237 [cite: 15]
* [cite_start]**Attrition Rate:** 16.12% [cite: 16]
* [cite_start]**Average Age:** 36.92 Years [cite: 17]
* [cite_start]**Average Salary:** 6.50K [cite: 18]
* [cite_start]**Average Years at Company:** 7.01 Years [cite: 3]

---

### ** Major Insights**
* [cite_start]**High Attrition Roles:** Laboratory Technicians (62) [cite: 79, 92] [cite_start]and Sales Executives (57) [cite: 80, 91] have the highest turnover counts.
* [cite_start]**Age Group Trends:** Employees aged **26-35** are the most likely to leave, accounting for 116 attrition cases[cite: 21, 65].
* [cite_start]**Education Factor:** The highest attrition comes from employees with **Life Sciences (38%)** [cite: 34] [cite_start]and **Medical (27%)** [cite: 27] backgrounds.
* [cite_start]**Gender Breakdown:** Attrition is significantly higher among **Males (143)** [cite: 4] [cite_start]compared to **Females (80)**[cite: 5].
* [cite_start]**Salary Impact:** A large portion of attrition occurs in the **Upto 5k** salary slab[cite: 25, 35].

---

### ** Technical Stack**
* [cite_start]**Tool:** Power BI Desktop [cite: 6]
* **Data Cleaning:** Power Query (ETL process)
* **Analysis:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for measures like Attrition Rate and Salary Slabs
* **Visuals:** Bar Charts, Donut Charts, Tree Maps, and KPI Cards

---

### ** How to Explore**
1. **Download:** Get the `.pbix` file from this repository.
2. **Open:** Use **Power BI Desktop** to view the interactive report.
3. **Interact:** Use the built-in filters to analyze data by Department, Job Role, or Education.
